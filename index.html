About Me

You do important and meaningful work for social change. I code. It's a match! My name is Firas Nasr and I am a full stack developer and human rights activist. After studying Human Rights at Middlebury College and working for a number of non-profit, social justice, and human rights organizations, I noticed a need for visually-compelling, websites and web platforms that meaningfully convey the important work being done by these organizations to change the world for the better. Allow me to support you or your organization in telling the story of your work so you can focus on doing the work itself.
